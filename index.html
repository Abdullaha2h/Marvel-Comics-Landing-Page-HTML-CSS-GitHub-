Marvel Comics FAQs

What is Marvel Comics?
Marvel Comics is a comic book publishing company known for creating iconic superheroes such as Spider-Man, Iron Man, and the Avengers.
Who are some popular Marvel characters?
Some popular Marvel characters include Spider-Man, Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Hulk, Black Widow, and Wolverine.
How can I start reading Marvel Comics?
You can start reading Marvel Comics by visiting your local comic book store, purchasing comics online, or subscribing to digital comic book services.
